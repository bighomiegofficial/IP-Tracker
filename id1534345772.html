Description

Don't let a big bank make you feel small. GO2bank™ is the banking app built for everyday people. Keep more of your hard-earned money with no monthly fees¹ and our free nationwide ATM network⁷.

No surprise fees
Say no to monthly fees and hidden fees² and yes to keeping more of your money. No monthly fees with eligible direct deposit¹. Otherwise, $5 per month.

Early paydays and more ways to save
When it comes to payday, early is right on time. Get your pay up to 2 days early and government benefits up to 4 days early with direct deposit⁶. Need cash? Withdraw for free at our free nationwide ATM network⁷. Want to save? Earn more with an APY well over the national savings rate average¹¹.

Built-in backup
Stay covered with up to $200 in overdraft protection after eligible direct deposits and opt-in³.

Credit building
With the GO2bank Secured Visa® Credit Card, climbing to better credit is easier with no annual fee⁵, and no credit check⁴ when you apply.

Account protection
Keep your money safe with just a tap on the app with lock/unlock your card⁸. And we'll send fraud alerts if we ever see anything shady¹⁰.

A bank you can trust
We're a Member FDIC bank and a brand of Green Dot Corporation, which means your money is federally insured⁹ and never held by a third-party bank. Green Dot has also been named one of Newsweek's Most Trusted Companies in America for 2023 and has serviced over 67 million accounts over the last 23 years.
